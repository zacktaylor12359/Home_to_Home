Welcome to Home to Home of Northville

Home to home is the best place in Northeville to buy and sell good quality consignment furniture and accesories at a fair price.

For those who are moving, downsizing, remodeling, or just looking to donate furniture and other household items, consign with us! You can rest assured knowing your items will be handled, priced and marketed with the deepest consideration of YOU, the consigner.

Terms an Conditions

Consignment split is 50/50.

The Consignment period is 60 days.

After the first 30 days, if your item has not sold, it will be reduced 20%. Home to Home will not reduce your items any lower than 20% during your consignment period.

Items not sold within 60 days must be picked up by the consignor within 10 days or they become the property of Home to Home to be disposed or donated (with the exception of FURNITURE).

We contact all furniture owners whose items have not sold after 60 days. If the seller requests, Home to Home may re-consign the furniture item for an addtional 60 days, but, at a lower price, generally 10%.

There is no waiting period for payouts. Payouts under $100 are in cash, over $100 by check.
